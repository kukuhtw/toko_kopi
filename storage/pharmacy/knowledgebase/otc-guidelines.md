# Pharmacy AI Knowledge Base

## General Rules

- AI chatbot is only for education and product information.
- AI chatbot is not a doctor.
- Hard drugs and antibiotics require pharmacist review.
- Recommend doctor consultation for severe symptoms.

## Fever

### Mild Fever

Possible OTC:
- Paracetamol
- Ibuprofen
- Vitamin C

Warnings:
- Fever > 3 days should consult doctor.
- Fever with shortness of breath requires immediate medical care.

## Flu

Possible OTC:
- Vitamin C
- Cough syrup
- Lozenges
- Honey syrup

Warnings:
- Persistent cough > 2 weeks should consult doctor.

## Gastric Issues

Possible OTC:
- Antacid
- Omeprazole
- Oralit

Warnings:
- Vomiting blood requires emergency care.

## Prescription Drugs

Examples:
- Amoxicillin
- Cefixime
- Ciprofloxacin
- Metformin
- Amlodipine

Rule:
- Require pharmacist verification.
- Require doctor prescription.
